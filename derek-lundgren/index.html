About

Derek Lundgren is a prominent real estate agent known for exceptional sales exceeding 100M in closed volume. He achieved recognition in 2021 as one of the top 40 under 40 agents, showcasing his dedication and ability to deliver exceptional results. Alongside his role as an accomplished agent, Derek is the visionary founder of a thriving Hazard Disclosure Company, a venture that empowers homebuyers by providing comprehensive insights into potential natural hazards affecting properties.

Derek's educational journey led him to graduate with a degree in Business Administration from Cal State San Marcos, equipping him with the strategic acumen necessary to navigate the complex real estate landscape. His passion and ambition go beyond the boardroom.

An adventurer at heart, Derek finds solace in exploring the outdoors through invigorating hikes and maintaining a dedicated fitness routine at the gym. His appetite for life extends to the culinary realm, where he savors the experiences of new restaurants and local San Diego breweries.

Beyond the hustle and bustle, Derek cherishes moments of tranquility. He's often found basking in the company of his loyal bulldog, Chubs. This connection reminds him of the simple joys in life and the importance of balance.

Derek's love for travel fuels his curiosity to explore diverse cultures and landscapes, although the allure of relaxation at home is equally appealing. In essence, Derek Lundgren is not only a skilled real estate professional but also a multifaceted individual who thrives on adventure, knowledge, and the pursuit of a harmonious life.
