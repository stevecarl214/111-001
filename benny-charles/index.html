About

Benny Charles is a 17-year real estate industry veteran with an unmatched reputation for excellence and integrity. Recognizing that one of life’s most difficult undertakings is the sale or purchase of a home, Benny is committed to helping his clients navigate the process—as a focused listener, able advisor, and fierce yet fair advocate. His direct, honest approach and keen market knowledge ensure clients feel confident during every transaction, while his diligent follow-through has earned him repeat customers and referrals.

A Southern California native, Benny enjoys spending time with his wife and four children and experiencing all the cultural and recreational opportunities the San Diego area provides. In his free time, he likes working out and fishing, and he is also a devoted volunteer at his church.
